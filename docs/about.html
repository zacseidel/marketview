What is this?

marketview is a personal hobby project — a stock screening and model-tracking tool built for fun and learning. It runs a handful of systematic models against the S&P 500 and S&P 400 universe twice a week and publishes the results here.

The models range from simple rule-based screens (momentum, buyback yield, mean reversion) to a gradient-boosted machine learning model trained on ten years of price and earnings history. None of this constitutes a trading system or a portfolio — it is exploratory research only.
